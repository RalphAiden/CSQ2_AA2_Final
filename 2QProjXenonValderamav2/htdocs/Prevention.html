The best way to protect yourself from a fire is to not let
the fire start. To do this, you first need to make sure
there isn't any flammable items near things like stoves
as it can start and make a fire spread. Then, you should
always check electrical and gas appliances. Leaving these
appliances on for an extended period of time can spark a
fire in you household. Lastly, install smoke alarms,
smoke alarms can help you detect a fire that has just
started so it can be stopped before it spreads and creates
a disaster.
