There are many ways to protect you and those around you
whenever there is a fire. First, you should know the escape route
from wherever you are to the evacuation center. You should also
put fire extinguishers in accessible places and install sprinklers
beforehand, but only Sif you have enough budget to afford them.
Second, NEVER use the elevators in any circumstance if you there
is a fire. Use the stairs and always stay low or crawl to make
breathing easier.
